About me

My name is Christian. I am 26 years and studying multimedia design at KEA. This page contains a selection of projects that I have been working on for the last year, and then some video material from my Højskole.

Madstitch

Full site here

The Madstitch site was an exam project for a danish textile artist, which also means that it is a more complete project than the others on this site because it was meant to replace the original site. The backend was set up so the client could control the content through wordpress.

Kalklandet

Full site here

Kalklandet was a school project with a focus on the structure of information. Three museums where coming together under 1 digital solution called Kalklandet and needed a more intuitive structure of information. The limited amount of time meant that we had to focus on the main flaws from our perspective, instead of finishing the whole site.

The football club

The football club was a redesign for an existing american football club which needed a more appealing front image to recruit more members and give insights about the club.
